**“IF YOU CAN’T FEED A HUNDRED PEOPLE THEN JUST FEED ONE” – MOTHER TERESA  **
When: **Saturday April 8, 2023**
Time: **9:00am-11:00am**
Where: **Community Ambulance Company**
**420 Lakeland Avenue, Sayville NY 11782**

The Community Ambulance Company partnering with United States Congressman Andrew Garbarino, New York State Assemblyman Jarett Gandolfo, Suffolk County Legislator Anthony Piccirillo, Islip Town Councilman Jim O’Connor and Islip Town Councilman John Lorenzo will be collecting much needed non-perishable canned and packaged food for the Greater Sayville Food Pantry for the Easter holiday.
With Easter just around the corner, it’s important to remember the significance of giving to those less fortunate and also a time to reflect on the blessings in our lives and extend a helping hand to those in need.
The drop off location is conveniently located outside in the front of the building parking lot, making it easy and convenient to drive up and drop off items without exiting your vehicle.
This food will assist the Sayville Food Pantry mission of feeding hundreds of families from our community that are in need.
Take a photo of the QR code with your phone to learn about Community Ambulance or to join our team of dedicated volunteers.  The web site is embedded into the flyer photo, you can hold your finger on the QR code to be directed.
