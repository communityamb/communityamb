## What's in it for you?

There's something truly special about lending a hand when someone needs it most, especially when volunteering. You don't need any medical background to start. We offer a great chance to dive into the medical world and enjoy these additional benefits:

- All medical training provided: CPR/AED certification as well as NYS EMT certification
- Attend Suffolk County Community College for Free – SERVES Program
- All uniforms provided
- A modest retirement program: Earn $30+ per month/per year of service after retirement!
- 10% property tax reduction for active members
- Opportunities to further your medical education through various training courses
- Opportunities to become an Advanced Life Support provider such as a Paramedic
- Form lifelong friendships
- An unmatched adrenaline rush: Imagine saving someone's life or delivering a baby

## Regular Membership

- All are eligible to apply for Company membership provided they are found to be of good moral standing, have not been convicted of a crime or have criminal charges pending against them, and are over the age of eighteen
- Required to ride a minimum of 6 hours of in-house standby each week.
- Upon full membership have voting rights within the company and right to hold office

![](/assets/images/uploads/2023/09/community-ambulance-new-logo.webp)

## Associate Membership:

- All are eligible to apply for Company membership provided they are found to be of good moral standing, have not been convicted of a crime or have criminal charges pending against them, and are over the age of eighteen
- Any fireman, EMS, or police personnel who has served 10 years or more of active service, with a recommendation from their previous Chief/Supervisor and a qualified driver or any person that has held a clean NYS Driver's License for over 20 years
- Not eligible for voting rights or to hold office within the company

## VOLUNTEER TODAY!

## Who are we looking for?

- Members of the community
- Persons eager to learn new medical skills
- Persons new to the medical field or already certified as EMT's or Paramedics

## What do I have to do?

- Be available for 6 hours a week for in-house standby
- Complete required trainings

## What's in it for you?

No medical experience is required, and this company offers an opportunity to join the medical field. Other benefits include:

- All medical training provided: CPR/AED certification as well as NYS EMT certification
- College tuition assistance – SERVES Program
- All uniforms provided
- Retirement program: Earn $30+ per month/per year of service after retirement!
- Property tax reduction for active members
- Opportunities to become an Advanced Life Support provider such as a Paramedic
