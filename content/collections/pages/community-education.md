As your EMS provider, we feel that it is our responsibility to contribute to our communities not only by providing a high level of emergency care, but also by providing important health and safety knowledge for the community. We enjoy the privilege of providing educational support and training to families, at School Fairs, Community events, and tours of our building.

We welcome anyone who wishes to learn more. For questions and scheduling please reach out to our secretary at [secretary@communityamb.org](mailto:secretary@communityamb.org)

## What We Offer

- Car seat safety inspections and child identification cards
- Hands-only CPR training for the public
- AED awareness and demonstration
- Narcan and opioid overdose response training
- Water safety and life jacket fitting
- Medication safety and proper disposal
- Identity theft prevention and document shredding
- School fair and community event appearances
- Building tours for youth groups and schools
