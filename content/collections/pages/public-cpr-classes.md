Community Ambulance is in a never ending flux of teaching CPR classes for the community. Hands only CPR classes have been paired with our prevention programs, while Heartsaver and Provider level classes are taught monthly at our Headquarters. CPR initiated in the first few minutes of a cardiac arrest can double or triple a person's chance of survival. Saving another persons life can be an unexpected and rewarding experience. Community Ambulance Company offers **FREE** CPR and AED training upon request.

For more information on availability of CPR classes please contact [secretary@communityamb.org](mailto:secretary@communityamb.org)

We also help place AEDs in local schools and businesses. [Learn about our AED program](/public-aeds).

Infant and child CPR is also covered in our [Car Seat Inspection events](/car-seat-inspection-hands-only-child-infant-cpr).
