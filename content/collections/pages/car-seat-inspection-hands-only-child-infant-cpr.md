## Car Seat Inspections & IDs

Community Ambulance Company has partnered with the Suffolk County Sheriff's Office to provide **FREE** car seat inspections and child CPR training. This is a great opportunity for new parents, baby sitters or anyone who wishes to be better prepared in the event of an emergency.

The set up is conveniently located in the front of the building, first floor, making it easy to flow through and expedite time. If you are getting your vehicle inspected you would pull into the drive through garage bay. Exit the vehicle and fill out a form while your car seat is being inspected. While you wait for it to be completed you will have the opportunity to learn Hands only child and infant CPR and have light refreshments. Once completed, drive through the other side of the garage and exit the location.

**Event Occurs: Annually in May**

At the Community Ambulance Company Headquarters
420 Lakeland Avenue in Sayville
