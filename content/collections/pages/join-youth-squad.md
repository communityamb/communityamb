Are you between 14 and 17 years old and interested in emergency medical services? Join the Community Ambulance Youth Squad!

Our Youth Squad meets every other Wednesday from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM during the school year. Members receive training in pre-hospital emergency care including patient assessment, CPR, bleeding control, fracture management, and oxygen administration.

At age 16, members become eligible for an ambulance observer program to experience emergency response firsthand.
