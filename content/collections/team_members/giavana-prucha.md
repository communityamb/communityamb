This year, she looks forward to returning to oversee the same Youth Squad program that shaped her, while also focusing on training the membership at large. In August of 2023, Giavana began pursuing her doctorate in medicine. When she is not training members or coordinating chaos, you can find her spending time outdoors with one (or all!) of her three dogs."
