His ascent to this role followed valuable experiences as Captain from 2020-2023 and Lieutenant in 2019. Before joining CAC, Dan contributed significantly to the Port Jefferson Volunteer Ambulance, reflecting his deep-rooted commitment to aiding others. In addition to his leadership duties, Dan shares his knowledge as an instructor in EVOC, CEVO, and Communications. His expertise also extends to serving as an Emergency Services Dispatcher and an EMT for various Suffolk County agencies. Dan's academic pursuits in Technical Theatre and Automated Lighting and Stage Rigging at Suffolk County Community College highlight his diverse interests. Away from his professional responsibilities, Dan enjoys photography, hiking, and spending time with his dog, Norman."
