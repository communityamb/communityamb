bio: "Marc embarked on his journey with Community Ambulance Company in April 1977. He swiftly achieved his EMT status and ascended the organizational ranks. By 1979, he was appointed Crew Chief of his duty shift, a role he passionately served in for over 25 years. His leadership prowess was recognized in 1984 when he was elected to the Board of Directors. By 1993, he took on the role of Treasurer, a position he continues to uphold. Between 2011 and 2014, Marc played a pivotal role in leading the committee responsible for designing and inaugurating the new ambulance headquarters, which was unveiled in October 2014. On a personal note, he met his beloved wife, MaryAnn, at the company. Now, with a marriage spanning 37 years, their legacy continues as their children, Colleen and James, are active members."
