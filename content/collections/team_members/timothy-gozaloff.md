bio: "It is with heavy hearts we announce the passing of Timothy Gozaloff. Tim graduated from Sayville High School, class of 1979. He retired in 2016 from the Suffolk County Police Department as a Detective Sergeant. Tim also spent a lot of his time volunteering in his community. He was a life member of both Community Ambulance Company and the Sayville Fire Department. Tim enjoyed traveling, playing golf, and spending time with his family.\n\nTim is survived by his wife of 39 years Diana, his son Timothy and his wife Stephanie, his daughter Jennifer Brady and her husband Ryan, his grandchildren Michael, Jonathan, Emma, and Ellie, his father Gerard of Sayville, his sister Barbara Gozaloff, his brothers Paul and his wife Jo-Ann, Michael and his wife Helen."
