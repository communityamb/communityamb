bio: "Mike Duffy began his journey with Community Ambulance Company in August 2005, quickly earning his EMT certificate the following year. Holding a Bachelor's degree in History from Adelphi, Mike's sense of duty led him to serve as a Lieutenant with the company in 2009. That same year marked another milestone for him as he joined the ranks of the NYPD. Juggling the responsibilities of a full-time police officer, Mike impressively secured a position as a top ten responder on three occasions. He once again took on the role of Lieutenant for Community Ambulance in 2017 and 2018. In his professional life, Mike continues to serve and protect as a Narcotics Detective with the NYPD."
