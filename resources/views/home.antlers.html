About Us

Founded in 1950, we've dedicated ourselves to the well-being of our community for over seven decades. Day or night, we're here, providing emergency medical services to 50,000 residents across Sayville, West Sayville, Bayport, Oakdale, and Bohemia within a 20-square-mile area. Our region is diverse, encompassing landmarks like Long Island MacArthur Airport, main highways, peaceful beaches, bustling commercial zones, quiet neighborhoods, and 15 schools in 3 school districts. Your safety drives us every single day.

Community Ambulance Company, Inc. of Sayville is a New York State Certified organization that was founded in 1950. Since then, the members of this company have provided high quality emergency medical care to the residents of five heavily populated towns in Suffolk County. Community Ambulance Company has always been a leader in the constantly changing world of EMS.

How Busy Are We?

Every alarm answered is a neighbor helped. From cardiac emergencies and car accidents to water rescues on Fire Island, our volunteers respond 24/7, 365 days a year — all at no cost to the communities we serve.
